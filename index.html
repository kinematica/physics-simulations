Kinematica

We're a couple of students in Columbia's Physics Department making fun physics demos and software tools for physics education. Our goals are to find elegant ways to illustrate nonintuitive phenomena through interactive physics demonstrations, and to make it easier for other people to make beautiful physics demos of their own.
